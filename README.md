# Analytics on Stock Market Data:

- Applied exploratory data analysis, statistical modeling, and data visualization techniques to high-value stock datasets, examining distributions and relationships across categorical and numerical features.


Columns/Descriptions (Detailed):
- Date (Date each stock values were recorded)
- Bitcoin price (The stock value of bitcoin at the current time recorded in USD)
- Bitcoin Volume (The trading Volume of bitcoin Stocks)
- Ethereum Price (Price of Ethereum in USD)
- Ethereum Volume (Trading Volume of Ethereum)
- Natural Gas Price (Price of natural gas in USD per million British thermal units)
- Natural Gas Vol (Trading Volume of natural gas)
- Crude Oil Price (Price of Crude Oil in USD per barrel)
- Crude Oil Vol (Trading Volume of Crude Oil Stocks)
- Meta Price (Stock price of meta in USD)
- Meta Volume (Trading Volume of meta stocks)
- Google Price (Stock price of Google in USD)
- Google Volume (Volume of google stock trading)
- Apple Price (Stock price of apple in USD)
- Apple Volume (Volume of apple stock trading)
- S&P_500_Price (Price index of the S&P 500)
- Nasdaq_100_Price (Price index of the Nasdaq 100)
- Nasdaq_100_Vol. (Trading volume for the Nasdaq 100 index)
